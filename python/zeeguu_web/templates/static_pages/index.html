Towards Your
Personalized Textbook

Find interesting texts at the right difficulty

{#

that are not too difficult for you

#}

Our system continuously searches the Internet for new texts based on your interests. It estimates their difficulty to present you with those in the zone of proximal development: not too hard to make you give up, but not too easy to bore you.

Unlike the generic texts destined for the average learner that you can find in textbooks, personally relevant texts will motivate you to study more. {# This in turn will result in you learning more.#}

Read with seamless support for translations

Studies show that you learn best when the texts that you read are a little challenging. This means that in the ideal text you will still encounter words that you don't understand.

By using machine translation our system helps you obtain translations in any text with a simple click (or tap on touch-enabled devices).

The system also provides word pronunciation support.

Optimally practice your personalized vocabulary

{#

#} {# Words that you once translated, go into your own#} {# private word list and are available#} {# from all your devices.#} {##} {#

#}

Based on your past interactions with texts the system uses machine learning a model of your vocabulary knowledge. It then generates a variety of vocabulary exercises, some of which, even use the original context of the word because contextual learning works better.

Spaced repetition algorithms optimize your practice and frequent words are prioritized. {# It also prioritizes words by their importance in the learned language.#}
