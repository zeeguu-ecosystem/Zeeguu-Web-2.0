Zeeguu

A research project designed to speed & fun up vocabulary learning in a new language

{% if not g.user %}
{% endif %}

Our approach is designed around three fundamental principles

Discover texts you like and that are not too hard

{#

that are not too difficult for you

#}

{# Unlike most traditional learning materials based on pre-defined,#} {# standardized and let's admit it: boring texts, #} We think that you will be more motivated if you can read only the stuff that you like in the language that you are learning. Blogs, articles and news will be recommended to you matching your favorite topics as soon as they appear on the net.

In all these contexts and on any chosen device, you will have information about text difficulty, such that you don't get demoralized by reading textst that are too difficult.

Read easily with one-click-translations everywhere

The texts that you read should not be too easy. Instead they should be "in the zone of proximal development": a bit challenging, so you improve. Thus, when you need to translate words, you should obtain translations at the touch of a fingertip.

Words that you have once translated, will go into your own, private dictionary and will be available to you always from all your devices. {# The information about the context where you#} {# encountered a given new word will also be synchronized across#} {# applications and devices.#} {# There is no doubt we understand and remember#} {# so much better words in context.#}

Practice vocabulary smartly in a personalzed way

Based on the texts you read, the words you translate, and lists of word frequencies, Zeeguu will use machine learning to build a model of your current vocabulary in the target language.

Applying this model and using spaced repetition, you will practice and retain the new words, ranked according to their importance.
